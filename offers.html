About

Description Hotel Felicity is located in the heart of Nairobi in an extremely characteristic, quite and lively area within short walk distance to all sites and is surrounded by the extraordinary beauty of churches, buildings, shops and monuments. Felicity Hotel is part of a lovingly restored 1900 palace. On entering this charming hotel in Nairobi, you will immediately sense its special intimate atmosphere that makes you feel like being in your own home. Each detail has been passionately chosen and each room deserves a visit. Hotel Felicity style mixes valuable antiques and original artworks with an unexpected eclectic contemporary twist. The entire House recalls the ancient ‘Palazzi’ of times past where young Arab aristocrats lived while discovering the beauty and the artistic mysteries of Kenya. The special charm and the cosy mood of Felicity will make you feel at home in Kenya.
